Instructions:

The game is pretty easy, a player can hit the moving hockey with
his paddle in order to direct it in the opponent's goal and vice versa.

Any player can play the game in two modes:
	1. The first is the single mode by which he can play V.S an AI.
	2. The other one he can play V.S another player.

Controls:

In case of the first mode:
The player can move the hockey with the arrow keys or the mouse.

In case of the other mode:
Either of the two players can play with the arrow keys or press:

	1. W: To move the hockey up.
	2. S: To move the hockey down.
	3. D: To move the hockey right.
	4. A: To move the hockey left.
